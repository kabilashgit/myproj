About Interior Planning Service

Whether you work in hospitality, retail, education, residential construction, or a corporate office, we can help you create smart spaces with our Interior Planning Service. From measurements to interior design, our experts are here to help.
This service will cost you just Rs 5000 and will be fully refunded if you buy IKEA products and solutions worth Rs.1,00,000 or more. Become an IKEA Business member and book our Interior Planning Service today!

STEP 1

Book a session

Help our experts understand your business needs by filling our booking form

STEP 2

Choose your plan

Join us at our store, on the date of your booking, to take a look at the plan we create especially for your business

STEP 3

Roll your plan out

Within the next two weeks, our team of experts will send you an e-mail with a detailed plan, keeping your preferences and budget in mind

Points to be noted
  • This planning service is only for IKEA Business members.
  • This service does not include pick-up, delivery or assembly.
  • We will not be able to meet you on the day that you make a booking. It takes us a few days to create a plan that suits your needs.
  • Registration closes 3 days before appointment date
  • Products you request for, or those suggested by our team, may not be available while we plan.
  • We recommend booking a session with us a few weeks before you need to be ready for your clients.
  • Allow 7-10 days for our planning team to create the detailed layouts
