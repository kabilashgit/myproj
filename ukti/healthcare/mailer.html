On 6 February 2018, the India-UK Createch Summit will be held in Mumbai. This one-day conference will celebrate the relationship between creativity and technology.

 
   
Healthcare will be a key thematic focus of the day and we will convene senior leaders and stakeholders from Mumbai’s hospitals, medical colleges and government institutions to discuss the latest technologies emerging from the UK and opportunities for collaboration with its leading institutions.  
   
As we celebrate 70 years of the UK’s National Health Service (NHS), and look to the decades ahead, we will examine how the NHS is transforming healthcare, through developments in science, technology, data and by using virtual reality. We will explore how the use of creative technologies will improve medical services for millions of people and how this learning can be applied to India.  
   
HRH The Prince Edward, Earl of Wessex KG GCVO, will be in attendance as our special guest.
