On 6 February 2018, the India-UK Createch Summit will be held in Mumbai. This one-day conference will celebrate the relationship between creativity and technology.

 
   
Industrial design will be a key thematic focus of the day and we will convene senior leaders and stakeholders from automotive, automation and design firms, and government and business institutions, to discuss the latest technologies, and explore opportunities for collaboration and partnership between the UK and India.  
   
Join us for an exciting day of discussion, debate and networking with top Indian and UK companies looking for collaboration and partnership.  
   
HRH The Prince Edward, Earl of Wessex KG GCVO, will be in attendance as our special guest.

  The industrial design sessions will examine the emerging and disruptive technologies changing the face of the sector, asking what’s next for industrial and product design. For example, what opportunities and challenges will artificial intelligence and virtual, augmented and mixed reality present to the design engineering sector?
