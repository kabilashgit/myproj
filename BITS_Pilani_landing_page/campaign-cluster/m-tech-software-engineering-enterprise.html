M.Tech. in Software Engineering is a four-semester Work Integrated Learning Programme designed for working professionals, The nature of software has evolved from small, single user systems to complex, multi-user systems catering to needs such as Retail Banking, Supply chain management, Telecom service management, etc. Engineering such systems requires newer capabilities over and above application of software technology.
Only those working professionals who are located at Bengaluru, Delhi NCR, Hyderabad and Pune can apply to this programme.

SALIENT FEATURES

Education Delivery at Employer's Premises

These programmes are delivered by experienced faculty at the partner organisation's own premises and can be pursued by the employees without taking any career break.

Industry Relevant & Rigorous Curriculum

WILP programmes are designed to integrate modern academic content with evolving requirements of the workplace and are delivered with the same rigour as programmes offered on campus.

Customised Curriculum

Designed to cater to the learning needs of specific industries, these programmes are also offered with a degree of customisation to suit the Learning & Development needs of corporate partners.

Assignments / Dissertation drawn from the work environment

Participants apply knowledge and concepts to address complex, real-world business issue.

LEARING METHODOLOGY

EXPERIENTIAL LEARNING

The programme emphasises on Experiential Learning that allows learners to apply concepts learnt in classroom in simulated and real work situations. This is achieved through:

Simulation based exercises

  • Simulation Software like NS2, Tossim, Net-SNMP, MultiSim, CPU-OS Simulator,Cheddar, Keil, CCS Studio
  • Design & Modelling Tools like StarUML
  • Programming Environments like Eclipse,Code::Blocks, Amazon Cloud, Android Studio
  • Other software/ tools like SQLite, Wireshark, Weka, OpenProj

Case Studies and Assignments: Carefully chosen real-world cases & assignments are both discussed and used as problem-solving exercises during the programme

Dissertation/ Project Work: The fourth semester offers an opportunity for learners to apply their knowledge gained during the programme to a real world like complex project. The learner is expected to demonstrate understanding of vital principles learnt across semesters and their ability to successfully apply these concepts
